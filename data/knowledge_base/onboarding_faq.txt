CloudOps Desk Onboarding FAQ

Q: How do new users join a workspace?
A: A workspace administrator sends an invitation from Workspace Settings > Members. Invites expire after 7 days.

Q: Can a user belong to multiple workspaces?
A: Yes. Users can switch workspaces from the account menu after accepting invitations.

Q: What roles are available?
A: Viewer, Analyst, Operator, Administrator, Billing Administrator, and Workspace Owner. The Workspace Owner role has final authority for ownership and security decisions.

Q: How do we import assets?
A: Administrators can upload CSV files from Assets > Import. CSV files must include asset_name, owner_email, environment, and criticality.

Q: How long does initial setup take?
A: Small teams usually finish initial setup in under one hour. Enterprise setup with SSO, SCIM, audit exports, and custom integrations may take several days.
