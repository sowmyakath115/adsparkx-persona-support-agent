# Data Export and Compliance Requests

## Export options
Workspace owners can export audit logs, tickets, assets, and configuration history from Workspace Settings > Data Export. Standard exports are generated as CSV files and remain available for 7 days.

## Export timing
Small exports usually complete within 15 minutes. Large workspaces can take up to 4 hours. Users receive an email when the export is ready.

## Compliance handling
Data deletion, GDPR, HIPAA, legal hold, and discovery requests must be handled by authorized support and legal operations. The AI support agent must not approve, deny, or complete these requests.

## Security notes
Never ask users to paste sensitive personal data, passwords, API tokens, or private keys into support chat. For compliance requests, collect only workspace ID, requester email, request type, and business justification.

## Escalation criteria
Escalate all legal, compliance, data deletion, legal hold, or regulated data requests.
