# SLA and Support Priority Policy

## Priority levels
P1 is a production outage or severe security event. P2 is major degradation with business impact. P3 is a standard product issue with workaround. P4 is a how-to question, cosmetic issue, or feature request.

## Initial response targets
Enterprise P1: 30 minutes. Enterprise P2: 2 business hours. Business P2: 4 business hours. P3: 1 business day. P4: 2 business days.

## Resolution guidance
Support response targets are not guaranteed resolution times. Resolution depends on root cause, customer environment, third-party provider status, and whether engineering changes are required.

## Escalation path
Escalate to human support for P1/P2 business impact, executive requests, production downtime, security events, or cases where the customer explicitly asks for a support manager.
