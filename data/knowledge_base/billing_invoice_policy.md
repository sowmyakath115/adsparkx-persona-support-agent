# Billing and Invoice Policy

## Invoice availability
Invoices are generated on the first day of each billing cycle and are available under Workspace Settings > Billing > Invoices. Only workspace owners and billing administrators can view invoices.

## Failed payments
If a payment fails, the workspace enters a 7-day grace period. During the grace period, users can continue using the product, but owners will see payment reminders. After the grace period, write operations may be restricted until payment is completed.

## Refund policy
Refund requests must be reviewed by the billing team. Support agents should not promise refunds, credits, discounts, or billing reversals. Refund eligibility depends on contract terms, subscription plan, usage, and local regulations.

## Invoice disputes
Invoice disputes require the invoice number, workspace ID, billing contact email, and a short description of the disputed charge. Do not ask customers to share full payment card numbers.

## Escalation criteria
Escalate all refund requests, invoice disputes, unauthorized charge reports, contract questions, tax exemption requests, and changes to billing ownership.
